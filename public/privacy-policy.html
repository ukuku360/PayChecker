Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PayChecker collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use the service.

1. Information we process

  • Account identifiers needed for authentication and account management

  • Technical logs required for reliability, abuse prevention, and debugging

2. How we use data

  • Provide core features such as shift tracking, calculations, and exports
  • Improve product reliability, performance, and support quality
  • Enforce limits and security controls

3. Ads and third-party services

PayChecker may display advertising and may use third-party services for analytics, infrastructure, and payment operations. Third parties may collect information according to their own policies.

4. Data retention

We retain data as needed to provide services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary by data type and operational needs.

5. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect data. No internet transmission or storage system is guaranteed to be fully secure.

6. Your choices

  • You may request account deletion and data removal where legally permitted
  • You may control uploaded content by deleting it from your account

7. Policy changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@paychecker-six.vercel.app.
